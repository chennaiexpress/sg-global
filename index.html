PROVISION

We provide, at very competitive prices, high quality fruits & vegetables, both local & imported, to vessels calling at the various ports in Malaysia. Fresh local vegetables are sourced from our market on a daily basis. To preserve the fresh- ness of our produce & products, these items are warehoused in cool or frozen rooms. These goods are transported in refrigerated vehicles and delivered to the vessel at the berth. We also can arrange delivery at anchorage at the request and convenience of the vessels calling in Malaysia waters. Also on offer are all types of frozen meats, imported fish, cold cuts and frozen vegetables.

BONDED

We supply our customers with a good assortment of the best bonded store products such as Beers, Wines, Spirits, Cigarettes, Cigars, Chocolates, Liqueurs, Whiskies, Rums and Snacks. We offer both recognized brands and local brand options.

ENVIRONMENT

SLOPS, SLUDGE DISPOSAL 
Slops – ballast water and tank washing residues (5% of petroleum products freighted, including water)
Sludge – Fuel residues from engine room (1%-2% of bunker consumption)
Bilge Water- Mixture of oil and water accumulated at the bottom of the vessel
We offer Service will remove the oil, transport the oil for safe disposal and recycle the slop oil into usable products such as electricity production and cement kiln. The customer will then be issued with a safe disposal certificate with the total amount discharged.
